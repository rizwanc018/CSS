# Popover (auto)

Click the button to show a simple popover. It automatically closes when clicking outside.

Press Esc or click outside to close.

<button popovertarget="basic-popover">Show Popover</button>

    <p>Popover content here</p>
</div>

# Popover (manual)

This popover uses popover="manual" - it won't close automatically when clicking outside.

Manual Popover

This popover requires explicit show/hide actions. Perfect for important dialogs or confirmations!

<button popovertarget="manual-popover" popovertargetaction="show">Open</button>
<button popovertarget="manual-popover" popovertargetaction="hide">Close</button>

<div id="manual-popover" popover="manual">
     <p>Manual popover content</p>
</div>
